## Sources
{:toc}

This is the extremely subjective end of "knowledge". The articles in the sub menus below consist of:

- Random reports of the woo, such as the Mandela Effect, possible real astral travel, lucid dreaming etc. taken from my own experience and other users on the old forum posted from 2016 onward.

- Personal wild morning dreams of substance starting in early May 2021.

- Visions in the waking state, some through meditation practices and some a continuation of the morning dreams while awake.

- Meditations exploring and then transforming the soul, then mind and then god (pure awareness) that could be described as a kind of remote sensing. This is a personal meditation diary starting from early May 2022 onward.

I believe all of the above, especially my own meditations, hold some rare insights into who we are, where we are and what this world is all about. "Outside" reality and personal identity is infinitely bigger than what I had imagined.

### The beginning
{:toc}

It all started in **early May 2021** when I felt some elements of my heart center being repaired or "switched on". It was like someone had flipped a switch and certain emotions from other realities rushed in over the next two to three days. That area felt completely different than before. Previous to this date I had had only one or two interesting dreams of note.

After May 2021, I also had continuous morning dreams of the same realities that sometimes bled into concrete day dreams during my waking hours that I had partial control of, including communication with other people in the dream.

The first reality bleed-through was what a lot of us think of as the "astral plane". Terminology can be misleading though. Strictly speaking, the astral area is the soul (my soul) and consists of an incredible near infinite number of spaces, universes and layers of which the reality in the concrete day dreams is but one absolute tiny, and I mean tiny, part of the soul, in which we reside.

{: .important-title }
> Edit: 09 january 2023
> 
> Yes, we on earth are living in the anchor of our soul. We contain all the soul energies and beings in us and around us, connected to us. That is why "the earth" is so important. In reality, there is no outside or inside. Everything is an energy construct woven together. If you have access to mind space, then you can command it all!
> 
> The soul (or my soul) is one of a near infinite number of energy spheres created in mind space by human-like energy beings (operators) in order to obtain energy from data, lived; and mind space is merely a **tool** of pure creation. Outside that is god space ("pure" consciousness in its own infinite spaces). Now you understand the ridiculous vastness of it all.
> 
> This first plane beyond the physical is perhaps best called the pure energetic realm rather than the "astral plane". It is where our phantom bodies ("spirit" body doubles) go when dreaming. These are exact copies of the physical body.

The reality from the concrete day dreams seems to be located right next door to the physical earth before the light worlds; although the soul is constantly changing and never remains static, especially in recent times. There are three tell-tale characteristics of this fluid space:

1. nything could be made into reality by thinking of it. This included objects, familiar surroundings and traveling to other places, flying etc. It was a pure energy realm of your imagination. For example, you could make your own private space based on a memory from the waking world.

2. The dream body was a carbon copy of the local physical one, even down to its flaws which included the slight discoloration on a small part of my physical body.

3. One major dream revealed four energetic realms close to one another at the time. It showed a mostly red/yellow energetic squashed energy sphere shaped like a vertical rugby ball leaning to the left a little. To the right of that pressing against it was a smaller navy/brown squashed energy sphere. 

	Both represented the pure energy world; although the red/yellow one was overlaid on top of the physical earth. These two areas contained the "phantom body" which we often go to when we sleep. They can be quite Technicolor.

	These "rugby balls" were followed on the right side by a black void space then two separate and larger, but close, squashed spheres consisting of white light in which avatars of other identities resided. These spaces are very different now though, due to the meditations being very proactive of late.

## Meditation practices

Around **mid-July of 2021**, the meditation practices outlined by [Franz Bardon](https://mega.nz/folder/7IpVlY7J#0iGvHqIYH8sLTVdx9mcZhQ/folder/WBRgUZCS) in his well-known book [Initiation into Hermetics](/assets/pdfs/transcendentalism/Franz-Bardon-Initiation-Into-Hermetics.pdf) were rekindled. In fact, someone from [4chan/paranormal](https://boards.4channel.org/x/catalog) linked to this and other [useful books](https://mega.nz/folder/NfwXDYRS#uQnuEPDzoeheXiiHUZmZDA) on this subject, in case Bardon isn't your cup of tea. Here is an even bigger [list](https://mega.nz/folder/jlEwhYyJ#iK4mVC4y5iwk_cr3eIpX4g) published by [another poster](/assets/pdfs/transcendentalism/magick-resource-books.pdf). (You won't find 4chan on any search engine except on [presearch.org](https://presearch.com/) and [yandex.com](https://yandex.com/)).

I had practiced up to near the end of stage 4 in 2009/10. However, for some reason the results were a bit different at times, possibly because myself (and the world) were different. The practices were dropped due to either a misreading or the Mandela Effect.

By the very end of June 2021, I was compelled to fast for three days and start up again. This compelling was a great urge that would not go away. I assume it came from my higher (operator) energy body.

The book is split up into 10 stages and is quite bare-bones in parts, probably so as not to constrict the practitioner too much. I often found myself doing my own thing and that the practices were often just a way of disciplining myself to set time aside every day to practice.

Intellectual knowledge is the tiny tip of the iceberg for senses out of the body. Practice is 99 percent of meditation. After all, if you want to become a competent boxer, you don't read a book on boxing, you obviously practice it: running, weights, sparring etc. Meditation is no different.

The first four stages is a preparation to strengthen visualisation (mind) and enable purification (soul). They iron out the relatively minor creases. Your imagination is the key to everything greater than what you think you are. The book unlocked some practices I just knew how to do and it came naturally, such as "opening the conduit".

It will be the same for you. If you are attracted to this sort of thing, it is because a "higher" energy being closer to the source of consciousness is switching you on and drawing you to them. They want you to become them, because you already are them, you just don't know it yet. Yours and their consciousness need to become fully one again.

The end of stage four opened myself up to other beings I was connected to in higher energy worlds of the earth energy construct. They are actually just other avatars of my soul, i.e. my "soul group". This was just the beginning.

## Meditation diary

### Waking dreams (Bardon stage 4)

In **mid-March 2022**, I was opened again; this time it wasn't the immediate phantom body energy realm, but the next separate "world" above ours, the lowest light world. These experiences have been written down under the **Waking dreams** menu.

Until around August 2022, there were 12 earth light worlds or energy constructs or matrices above ours. Those light worlds have now been amalgamated. They all exist in my soul and it has been changing at a rapid rate. They are only a tiny, tiny part of the greater existence, of which you belong in total.

### Entering and transforming the soul (Bardon stage 5+)

In **late March 2022**, stage 5 was begun; whereas by mid-July 2022, stage 7 was partly completed; although I am doing my own thing **only** now. Stages 5 through 7 will switch you on. It is up to you how you transform yourself after that.

> Take note
> 
> When you reach stage 5 and begin to explore your solar plexus area clairvoyantly, don't stop as Bardon suggests. Instead, go microscopically into the energy shape or object you see before you, and **keep going!** You will go through half a dozen objects until you come out into a brand new universe... your soul.

My consciousness went from being in a tiny part of my head to all over my head to all over my body and beyond. These feelings of where your awareness resides is key to self transmutation and identity. It is a natural side effect. 

If stages 1 to 4 were a purification, stages 5 to 7 are an energising; although this also purifies at a more intense level. You are on your own after that. 

Each of these daily meditations was a quasi remote sensing exploratory and transformative visualisation and feeling of the myriad of universes within the soul, mind and god. They have been written down in an **unpublished** diary immediately after each meditation, which usually takes about 35 to 40 minutes these days. They will eventually be published under the **Entering the soul** menu and other menus below that.

Treat my transcendental writings as only a teaser. You must live it if you really want to transform your soul and empower god, not read about someone else doing it. **Energy is data, lived.** I hope these writings inspire you to become your "greater self" and help you become a cleaner and more powerful sovereign being.

Remember, there is nothing out there at all. It is literally all inside you, and always was, at least if you want to reconnect with yourselves. You will understand this more by stage 5.
