## Entering the soul ##

Here begins the journey of my consciousness through the energy centre of the solar plexus, as instructed by Franz Bardon in the book _Initiation into Hermetics_, stage 5. Bardon didn't mention going any deeper. I took it upon myself to go more and more microscopic until I entered an incredible universe I had never seen before. I was accompanied by mostly Star Trek-like vehicles through many universal layers until I was shown a world of trapped celestial beings in Matrix-like pods; all of which I freed. These universes were my soul and all the beings therein were a part of that, including the physical avatar writing these words.

The story starts off in forum format; in other words, I was writing like I was speaking to other members. It changes fairly quickly to a personal meditation diary, the writings of which were never published on the old forum. Some references you won't understand, like "+9 world", until I publish all the material under the Waking dreams menu.
