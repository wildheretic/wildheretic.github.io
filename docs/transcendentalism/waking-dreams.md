## Waking dreams ##

These are the semi-remote sensing visualisations that manifested from the meditation exercises of Franz Bardon stages 1 through 4, outlined in his book _Initiation into Hermetics_. They are mostly the auditory, visual and feelings bleed-throughs from other worlds close to ours via other avatars living their lives in these very different reality constructs.
