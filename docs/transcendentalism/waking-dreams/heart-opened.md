Wild Heretic wrote:  
Okay, this is very woo and took me by complete surprise. I have to write this down as I've never felt like this before.  
  
I felt the change around a couple of weeks ago (beginning of May 2021), maybe less than that actually. In real life, I started to have small clips of memories from dream world that were unknown to me previously in any dream I have had. They were triggered by events here. The memories were not fuzzy like a normal dream but had a crystal clear clarity.  
  
A couple of days after this I started to get very strong feelings in the solar plexus and just above it in the chest area. I started to feel really happy and joy and happiness verging on tears started to flood into me. I'm a very mental person and have been exercising this area for some time now. I have never felt anything like this before.  
  
I am feeling this right now, but it comes in waves of realization mixed with happiness and excitement. The second wave occurred last night. The first happened last week or so. It is intensifying. The energies of dream world are here and bleeding through.  
  
How far this will go I don't know, but it has already started and taken effect. If it goes all the way, then our lives and this world will be changed forever beyond recognition. I mean, the possibilities are endless. It may even mean a complete defragmentation of dream world conscious and this world consciousness, which will be beyond wild. If all memories come back then I'll be a right mess as we all are living two separate lives.  
  
Yesterday evening I mowed the lawn (takes a long time due to its size) and it was effortless. In fact everything is less or no effort now. My wife couldn't find a lost widget for the boot of her car. I went over to look and found it immediately sitting on the lip of the bottom right in front of her face. She couldn't believe she hadn't seen it as she had searched there for a while.  
  
The strimmer and lawnmower started instantly with the first pull of the cord. Never happened before, ever, not even close. It's small stuff like that, but it really matters. This is brand new. We're not in Kansas anymore.  
  
I feel it right now. Right now! Last night I started singing the _Mötley Crüe_ song "Home Sweet Home". I first heard of that song from Hot Tub Time Machine (great film). That song encompasses the same feeling. My wife said "You're singing. You never sing. Are you really happy?" I said, "Yes, I'm feeling really happy right now."

Something extremely major has changed and I'm sure I'm not the only one feeling this.
  
On a side note, I had a dream on Monday morning around 7 am GMT 10 May 2021 (all my dreams are in the morning usually, at least the good ones). I was in a high tech city. The place exists for real in dream world, of that I am sure. It is an international city, but has an American and Japanese feel to it. Very high tech with huge glass skyscrapers, lots of people milling around, wide pavements (boardwalks), arcade entertainment machines and fun parks that can only exist in dream world. I've been there several times before. It felt quite familiar.
  
Normally, I never dream of Americans. I think this is because we are asleep in different time zones and so never meet up. I also hardly ever meet famous people. In fact, I've only ever met six famous people in dreams my entire life before this episode, and they were all Europeans.
  
Anyhow, I'd come out of a fun park, which was really another world connected to this city (hard to explain), I was looking at the arcade machines at the exit behind a one pane glass window. They were white with flat screens and white joining plastics seats. I thought the game looked a bit boring so I walked around and came back. 
  
When I came back, there was William Shatner sitting in one of the machines and the rest were occupied (Americans who had just gone to bed and just arrived) except for one on his right side which had a toddler/baby sitting in it and a few very young kids milling around near William. I surmised they were his or his grand-kids. He took the baby off the machine so I could sit down, which was nice of him. 
  
I sat at the machine and because I was waking up, my waking mind and its memories were taking over. I couldn't remember his name. We were familiar with each other. Not friends, but we had a common interest in the mysteries of the world and the paranormal. It felt like we had met up a few times or gone to conferences in the dream world on this subject. 
  
I said, " Hi sergeant". I then remembered his name with my waking brain and said "Sorry William. I said sergeant because I was thinking of TJ Hooker." He didn't care and dismissed it quickly not being distracted from his game. He then leaned over and said "Don't miss the show this Monday and Tuesday night. We have some brand new information." Or something like that. I said "That sounds great." But I was thinking, I live in Ireland, I doubt they will show his new show at the same time as America. 
  
Then suddenly to my right the guy from Ferris Bueller's Day Off (FBDO) appeared before me standing up. I know right. I didn't know his real name or the character name in FBDO; yet, like William, we knew each other. It was as if this guy and William were research partners in the paranormal or something. It was the guy who accidentally reversed his dad's Ferrari through a plate glass window on to the trees below. I had to look him up after I had woke up and his name is Alan Ruck. 
  
I imagine he has grey hair these days, but he appeared to me as about 40 years old with dark brownish hair. He was grinning ear to ear looking at me and nodding saying something like "We've discovered something completely new, ground-breaking to do with the paranormal". 
  
I said, "A new angle?" and he just nodded and said don't miss the show on Monday and Tuesday night. Then they both went off to the guy who runs the arcade venue in the room behind the counter. I looked in my pocket and saw I had a 50p and 20p piece with some coppers. So I went over to the guy in the back room and was thinking asking him for 6 dimes, but then remembered I needed six 10p pieces for the video game, not dimes. 
  
Alan and William were with looking to talk to this guy to get change and there were lots of other people milling around and gathering. I got served first as I had a feeling I'd done this guy a big favour in the past a while back. He gave me three 10p pieces and a three shiny silver bottle caps that did the same job. I went over to the arcade machine and then I woke up. 
  
So I look up Alan and find that he had worked together with William on a Star Trek Movie.

In fact, his hair style and colour looked just the same as in that movie. His face was more glowing or colourful though. William's body was much less portly than usual and he had light to mid-dark short curly hair and looked around 50 years of age. I guess our dream body looks better than our physical one. Maybe I appeared to them as Adonis... Okay yeah, maybe not. 
  
They must know each other in real life. Maybe they have worked together investigating the mysterious in real life, or maybe its just the dream world. Who knows? I have thought a lot recently about my dreams because of these feelings and have concluded that in dream world we tend to stick together the most in terms of language. Then comes time zones, then ethnicity (I've only had two dreams with blacks, two with Mexicans, and one with British Asian Indians), then alive or dead people. 
  
There is still a decent overlap between the living and the dead, but the living tend to stick together, probably because culturally they have more in common. I have conversed with a couple of dead people in my now broken German however. I know they were dead because of their style and because they told me outright and when. The dead and living mix in dream world vacation places though and some cities have a variety of old and modern architecture throughout. 
  
A guy in a old-fashioned light-coloured suit once appeared before me speaking Latin. I had a waking brain and didn't understand him, but the woman in the same room as me spoke back to him in English as if she understood him. 
  
All I know is that the energies of dream world are here. They arrived at the beginning of May 2021 and I hope to keep anchoring them and feeling great. May they intensify. 
  
The process has begun. We could be in for the wildest ride yet.  ;) 
  
Oh yeah, whatever you do, don't take the vax. Got a feeling its real purpose is to stop people entering this new state of being. But, each to their own so to speak. Time to choose.
